Introducing Student Representative Council

Class A 24 is one of the first grouping classes of the 24th batch of Nahdlatul Ulama University, Blitar. In this class there are 35 students, 9 males, and 26 females. Like classes in general, class A24 also has a class structure. The structure of class A24 is divided into 5 divisions contained in the Student Representative Council. This council regulates all student activities, both inside and outside the classroom. The DPM structure itself is divided into 5 components which include, Chairperson, Vice Chairperson or Secretary, Treasurer, Advisor, and PJMK. They have their own duties. We will explain these duties in the next paragraph.

Duties of Each Division

The chairman is the head of the main division in the student representative council structure. His duties are to oversee the running of the government, create work programs, open meetings (internal and external), and manage student order. A chairman also has a mission, where the mission will be the benchmark in running a government. The secretary is the chairman's personal assistant in implementing programs and overseeing the running of the government. He is given the authority to check everything that is a class problem. The secretary also helps in solving problems that sometimes never get resolved. So, overall the secretary's job is to be an assistant to the chairman. The treasurer is also no less important in a structure. Finance, administrative problems, and cash are under the control of the treasurer. Recording the flow of money in and out, withdrawing cash, and assisting in class management. The advisor is a personal assistant who is tasked with providing solutions to class problems. This division is very important, because the information given will greatly influence the welfare of students. Usually the advisor is the oldest person in the class. The older, the wiser the solution given. PJMK is the person in charge of the class course. The task of PJMK is as a reference or representative of the lecturer to deliver the assignments given. PJMK is also involved in listening to the aspirations of students. Capturing every aspiration and conveying it to the internal division structure of DPM.

Conclusion

Class A24 is an elite class with high intellectuality. The structure of this class is very complex, starting from the chairman, vice or secretary, treasurer, advisor, and PJMK. Each division has its own role, the chairman as the head of government, the secretary as his assistant, the treasurer as the financial division, the advisor as the personal assistant to the chairman, and PJMK as the representative of the lecturer and the bearer of student aspirations. Of the many explanations above, this class is the coolest class with the best management in its time.
